About Casella

A respected name in office seating, built on quality and trust.

Since its establishment in 1997, Casella has earned a well-respected place in the domestic and international market through an expert team, a clear quality understanding and a customer-oriented approach.

Operating across the full office-chair spectrum, Casella meets every kind of workplace need through a wide product range, an extensive service network and authorized dealers, with serious contributions to the industry in R&D, patent, design and total excellence.

Why Casella

One manufacturer, from sketch to shipment.

In-house design & R&D

Every Casella chair is conceived, engineered and refined by our own design and R&D team, with patents and design ownership behind the range.

ISO 9001:2015 quality

Design, manufacture, sales and service all operate under a certified Quality Management System, identifying the brand with quality and trust.

Manufactured in Turkey

Production runs under one roof at our own facility, keeping quality, lead times and consistency firmly in our hands.

Global export network

Casella exports across Europe, the Middle East, Africa and Asia, backed by an extensive dealer and service network.

Ergonomic engineering

Project & contract ready
